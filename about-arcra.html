About the Directory

Built to Connect the RC Rally Scene.

ARCRA is building a national home for RC rally in the United States. The goal is simple: make local clubs, organizers, events, drivers, hobby shops, brands, and rally-focused companies easier to find.

Explore the Directory Add a Listing

Why ARCRA Exists

RC rally is growing in scattered pockets across the country, often through Facebook groups, hobby shops, parks, temporary courses, and small volunteer-run events.

One Place to Find the Scene

Many great RC rally groups are difficult to discover unless you already know somebody local. A club may only exist as a Facebook group, an Instagram account, a YouTube channel, or a recurring event post shared by word of mouth.

ARCRA brings those scattered pieces into one visible directory so new drivers can find nearby events, organizers can connect with each other, and the wider RC industry can see where rally is actually happening.

ARCRA Connects

  • RC rally clubs and local organizers
  • Drivers looking for places to race
  • Rallycross, stage-rally, and raid events
  • Hobby shops and RC-friendly venues
  • Manufacturers, parts companies, and brands
  • Media channels, creators, and sponsors

Who ARCRA Is For

The directory is useful whether you are looking for your first event, building a local series, or trying to reach the people actively growing RC rally.

Drivers

Find nearby clubs, event organizers, social groups, and places to drive. Learn which types of vehicles local groups run and use their pages to check current dates, locations, and entry details.

Clubs & Organizers

Get found by new drivers. Share your location, event style, social links, vehicle classes, and what makes your local rally scene different from everyone else’s.

RC Companies

Find active clubs, regional events, content creators, and grassroots communities that use and promote RC rally vehicles, tires, electronics, bodies, tools, parts, and accessories.

Where This Can Grow

The club directory is the starting point. Over time, ARCRA can become a more useful connection point for the entire RC rally ecosystem.

Club & Event Listings

A searchable map of local groups, recurring events, stage rallies, rallycross series, rally raids, and regional meetups across the country.

Brands & Products

A place for RC manufacturers, parts makers, tire companies, body makers, electronics brands, and other rally-friendly companies to be found by active drivers and organizers.

Shops & Venues

Listings for hobby shops, RC tracks, parks, facilities, and private venues that support or host RC rally activity.

Media & Partnerships

A way for YouTube channels, photographers, event sponsors, and creators to connect with organizers and communities that are actively building the sport.

What ARCRA Is Not

The goal is to help people find one another, not to control how every local club runs its events.

An Independent Directory

ARCRA does not set a national rulebook, require a membership fee, charge clubs to be listed, or claim ownership over anyone’s event. Every local group has its own terrain, vehicles, class structure, timing methods, and culture.

ARCRA exists to make those groups easier to discover and to give the people doing good work a bigger place to be seen.

Want to Be Listed?

Whether you run a club, organize an event, own a hobby shop, build RC products, or create rally content, ARCRA is looking for people who are helping grow the scene.
