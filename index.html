How ARCRA Works

This is not a sanctioning body or a paid listing site. It is a practical map of the RC rally scene as it grows.

01

Find a Group

Search clubs, event weekends, and organizers in your region. Use the social links to see current dates, locations, and vehicle rules.

02

Show Up

Every local scene runs differently. Some are timed stage rallies, some are rallycross, some are trail-oriented, and some welcome any RC vehicle.

03

Build the Map

Know another good club? Submit it. The goal is a directory that helps every local organizer get found by the next driver.

About ARCRA

One place for the scattered but growing RC rally community in America.

American RC Rally Association

ARCRA exists to make RC rally easier to find. Whether your local scene runs 1/10 scale rally cars, short course trucks, Mojave Groms, old Tamiya chassis, or something completely different, there is room in the directory.

Listings are free. A club does not need to be large or formal to be included. A Facebook group, Instagram page, YouTube channel, website, or recurring event is enough to get started.

What This Is Not

ARCRA does not set national vehicle rules, charge clubs to be listed, or claim ownership of anyone’s local event. It is simply a place to help drivers find the people already doing good work.

Know a Club We Missed?
