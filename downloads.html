How to Use These

Print the boards, cut them out, place them on your stage, and make your RC rally course feel like a real event.

Step 01

Print

Print the files on 18x24 corrugated yard sign board. This is the same style of material used for common yard signs and small event signs.

Step 02

Cut Out

Use a sharp razor or utility knife and a safe cutting surface. Cut out the individual billboards, arrows, finish boards, or the center opening of the start gate.

Step 03

Place Around Track

Mount the signs with H-stands, wire stakes, small wood supports, tape, clips, or whatever works for your terrain. Place them around the course as billboards, gate markers, and finish-line boards.

Important Note

These are fan-made printable course scenery files for RC rally use. Brand names and logos shown on the printable signs belong to their respective owners.

ARCRA is not claiming sponsorship, ownership, approval, partnership, or endorsement from the brands shown. The purpose is to create realistic-looking course scenery for hobby use.

For smaller 1/14 to 1/16 scale courses, these files can also be printed smaller, such as 12x18, as long as the artwork is not stretched or distorted.
